## 为什么开始写博客？

一直想有个地方把学到的东西沉淀下来。笔记软件太私密，朋友圈太碎片，博客刚刚好——写给未来的自己看，顺便如果能帮到别人就更好了。

## 打算写什么？

大概会围绕这几个方向：

- **网络安全**：渗透测试的学习记录、CTF 解题思路、静态分析笔记
- **GUI 测试**：自动化测试的工具和踩坑经历
- **LLM 工具**：用大模型做安全研究的一些实验和想法
- **随笔**：读书、生活、在新加坡的日常

不定期更新，但会努力保持～ 🌸

## Why start a blog?

I've always wanted a place to consolidate what I learn. Note-taking apps feel too private, social media too fragmented — a blog feels just right. Writing for my future self, and hopefully useful to someone else along the way.

## What will I write about?

Mainly around these areas:

- **Cybersecurity**: Penetration testing notes, CTF writeups, static analysis
- **GUI Testing**: Automation tools, frameworks, and lessons learned
- **LLM Tooling**: Experiments using large language models for security research
- **Life**: Reading, daily life, living in Singapore

Updates will be irregular, but I'll try my best 🌸
